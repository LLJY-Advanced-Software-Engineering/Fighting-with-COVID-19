Frequently Asked Questions

Find Answers You're Looking For. 

 

Where do you get your data?
This site combines data from many sources and presents it in a both spatial, tabular and visual form.
In "Resources" page, we provide data providers and many APIs.
Why are the data different than other health websites?
The data updates from various APIs , might not reflect some cases still being reported.
We make all efforts to keep this table up-to-dateIt but it is possible that some changes from sources produce imprecise / no data being reported for the several regions.
Why do your health region boundaries not match Statistics Canada?
We report our data at the health region-level.
The shape files used are the Regional Health Boundaries as prepared and maintained by the ESRI Canada Community Maps Team.
Why does the data not present in some webpages?
There is a small delay between data sources and our site.
Please wait patiently for a while.

Fighting with COIVD-19
