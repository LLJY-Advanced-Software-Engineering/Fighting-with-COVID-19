COVID-19 Tracker

Three services are provided to track and model the information of COVID-19 in different perspectives.

 

  Tracker-Global(line)  

  Tracker-Global(map)   

  Tracker-Canada(line)  

Click any icon or image to the services you want

FEATURES

COVID-19 Tracker-Global-Line

This service provide a line chart to record the confirmed cases, recovered cases and deceased from different countries in earth.

COVID-19 Tracker-Global-Map

This service provide a map to record the reported cases and deceased from different countries in earth. The map could present some hot dots in almost countries.

COVID-19 Tracker-Canada-Line

This service provide a line chart to record the confirmed cases, active cases, test cases, recovered cases and deceased for different provinces in Canada.
